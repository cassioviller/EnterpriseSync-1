#49 - Corrigir Custo do Orçamento: Técnico vs Compra

What & Why
The Orçamento currently reports custo_total using the commercial/purchase cost (custo_compra), calculated from rounded purchase/package quantities × price. This differs from the technical cost used by the Serviço catalog, which is Σ coeficiente × preço. As a result, the budget can show an artificially low cost and inflated profit.

The purchase/commercial quantities are useful for procurement planning, but they must not replace the official cost basis of the budget.

Current wrong behavior:
- Backend: services/orcamento_view_service.py calculates custo_unit as the technical cost, but assigns item.custo_total from custo_compra.
- Frontend: templates/orcamentos/editar.html calculates custoUnit and custoCompra, but uses custoCompra for the item cost summary.

Done looks like
- item.custo_total in the backend equals qtd_item × custo_unitario.
- The budget editor live-preview shows the same cost as the backend.
- lucro_total = venda_total − custo_total uses the corrected technical cost basis.
- quantidade_compra and subtotal_compra remain calculated and stored as auxiliary purchase/procurement data.
- The purchase subtotal columns may continue showing package/purchase information, but the official item/budget cost summary must use technical cost.
- Existing budget items are recalculated on next save/recalculation.

Out of scope
- Changing the Proposal PDF or portal display.
- Changing the pricing formula preco_unit = custo_unit / divisor.
- Removing purchase/package columns.
- Changing labels beyond what is needed to avoid confusion.

Steps
1. Backend:
   In services/orcamento_view_service.py, inside recalcular_item(), change:
   custo_total = custo_compra.quantize(...)

   to:
   custo_total = (qtd_item * custo_unit).quantize(...)

   Then keep:
   lucro_total = venda_total - custo_total

   Keep quantidade_compra and subtotal_compra in the snapshot as auxiliary purchase data.

2. Frontend:
   In templates/orcamentos/editar.html, change:
   const custoTot = Math.round(custoCompra * 100) / 100;

   to:
   const custoTot = Math.round(custoUnit * qtd * 100) / 100;

3. Tests:
   Run/update:

   - tests/test_orcamento_service.py
